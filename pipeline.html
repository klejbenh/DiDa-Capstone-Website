System Pipeline

From track analysis to playlist flow

01

Audio Feature Capture

Extract BPM, key, loudness, energy, and spectral signatures from each track.

02

Transition Feature Engineering

Measure tempo gaps, harmonic compatibility, and energy movement between pairs.

03

Pairwise Ranking Model

Train a boosting model to score how naturally one song should follow another.

04

Graph-Based Sequencing

Build a weighted song graph and search for a path with the strongest overall flow.
